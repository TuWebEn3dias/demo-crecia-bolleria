Lucrecia's oven

Behind every pastry, every croissant, and every cheese bread lies a story that begins with curiosity. Lucrecia Pérez Raggio studied gastronomy at UADE, trained with Argentina's finest pastry chefs, and spent years sharing her knowledge through teaching.

But her dream was to have her own space — a place where artisan pastry took center stage. In May 2024, that dream came true at Juncal 2179.

"Recoleta needed a place where pastry and baking were the stars. Every recipe here is made by hand, with attention to every detail."

Today Crecia is more than a coffee shop. It's the meeting point for lovers of great coffee, giant cinnamon rolls, "god-level" medialunas, and an XXL alfajor that could easily pass for a four-portion cake.
